In the V8 API, a field was added to [`PastOrder`](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v8/html/T_Resto_Front_Api_Data_Orders_PastOrder.htm) [`OriginName`](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v8/html/P_Resto_Front_Api_Data_Orders_PastOrder_OriginName.htm) field was added to the V8 API.

This field contains the source of the closed order from which it was received.
