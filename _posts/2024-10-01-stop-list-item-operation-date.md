title: Date and time of adding an item to the stop-list are now available
layout: default
tags: v9preview3 v9
---

Starting from V9Preview3, the API provides the ability to find out the date and time of adding an item to the stop-list: `DateTime? operationDate` in the method
[`GetStopListProductsRemainingAmounts`](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v9/html/M_Resto_Front_Api_IOperationService_GetStopListProductsRemainingAmounts.htm).
