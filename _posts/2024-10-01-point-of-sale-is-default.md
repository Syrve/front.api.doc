The [`IsDefault`](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v9/html/P_Resto_Front_Api_Data_Organization_IPointOfSale_IsDefault.htm) property has been added to [`IPointOfSale`](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v9/html/T_Resto_Front_Api_Data_Organization_IPointOfSale.htm).
This property reflects the "Default Fiscal Register" setting.
This information is needed when working in the mode of splitting orders by Cooking Place Type.
