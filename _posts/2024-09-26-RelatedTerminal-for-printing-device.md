In V9Preview3, the [RelatedTerminal](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v9/html/P_Resto_Front_Api_Data_Device_IPrintingDeviceInfo_RelatedTerminal.htm) field was added to the [IPrintingDeviceInfo](https://syrve.github.io/front.api.sdk/v9/html/Properties_T_Resto_Front_Api_Data_Device_IPrintingDeviceInfo.htm) interface.

This field contains the terminal to which this printing device is related. Otherwise, its value will be `null`.
